Why and How?

This app's objective is to help you meassure how much time you will need to wait to an event.

  • Are you worried about your next meeting?
  • Are you obsessed with New Year Countdown?
  • Do you want to know how much time do you have to study your next exam?

If you answered "yes" to any of these questions this app is for you.

Time Left will help you to take under control your time and planifications.

Add a name and a date and thats all.

Of course you will also find the typical options as remove/edit an event.
